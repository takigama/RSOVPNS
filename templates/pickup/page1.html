Token Pickup

Welcome to the token pickup page, here you must do things, most important ensure that you have:
  • Your mobile device
  • Google Authenticator installed on your device
  • A Barcode scanner (one is in-build to google authenticator)
  • If scanning the QR code from the desktop, ensure you have privacy
  • Token can only be retrived once, so if you make a mistake, a new token must be created.

    You may now either continue from this page from your desktop or mobile device once the above have been completed. When using the desktop you must scan the QR code that is presented in either a QR code reader on your device or via the Google Authenticator software. When using your mobile device you will simply have to click a link from the browser that should automatically install the token in the Google Authenticator software.
